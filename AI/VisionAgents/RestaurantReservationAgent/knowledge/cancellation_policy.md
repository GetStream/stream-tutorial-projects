# Reservation & Cancellation Policy — La Maison Stream

## Making a Reservation

- Reservations can be made by phone, or through our website at **www.lamaisonstream.com**.
- We accept reservations up to **30 days in advance**.
- A **credit card on file** is required for parties of 6 or more.
- Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability.

## Cancellation Policy

- **24-hour notice**: Reservations may be cancelled or modified free of charge up to **24 hours** before the reserved time.
- **Late cancellation (less than 24 hours)**: A cancellation fee of **$25 per person** will be charged to the card on file.
- **No-show**: Guests who do not arrive within **15 minutes** of their reservation time without notice will be marked as a no-show. A fee of **$50 per person** will be charged.

## Large Party & Private Event Policy (8+ guests)

- A **non-refundable deposit of $200** is required at the time of booking.
- Cancellations made **48 hours or more** in advance: the deposit may be applied to a future reservation within 60 days.
- Cancellations made **less than 48 hours** in advance: the full deposit is forfeited.

## Modifications

- Changes to party size, date, or time can be made free of charge with at least **24 hours' notice**, subject to availability.
- Reducing party size by more than half within 24 hours may incur a **$15 per removed guest** fee for large party bookings.

## Special Circumstances

- We understand that emergencies happen. If you need to cancel due to an emergency, please call us directly and we will do our best to accommodate you.
- Weather-related closures or cancellations by the restaurant will receive a **full refund** or complimentary rebooking.
